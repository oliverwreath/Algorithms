  • Bubble Sort !
  • Insertion

Trees:

  • Binary Search Tree -- Prioritized Queue/ Dictionary

double LinkedList

  • addAfter, addBefore, addFirst, addLast, find, findLast, clear, contain, remove

Others:

  • Stack: rotated Stack
  • Queue: by 2 Stacks
  • Staque: Queue made by 2 Stacks
